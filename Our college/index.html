Course We Offer

We will offer six courses for student by Central Campus of Technology

BIT

The BIT course is a full time four years (eight semesters) Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology.This degree is primarily focused on subjects such as software, databases, and networking.

B.Sc.

The B.Sc. course is a full time four years Bachelor’s Degree in Science. This degree is primarily focused on subjects such as Physics & Biology.

BSC.CSIT

The BSC.CSIT course is a full time four years (eight semesters) Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science & Information Technology. This degree is primarily focused on subjects such as software, databases, and networking. Same as BIT.

B.Tech(Food)

The B.Tech(Food) course is a full time four years Bachelor’s Degree in Food Technology. This degree is primarily focused on subjects food science.

B.Sc.Nutrition and Dietetics

The B.Sc.Nutrition and Dietetics course is a full time four years (eight semesters) Bachelor’s Degree in science of Nutrition and Dietetics.This degree is primarily focused on subjects Nutrition and Dietetics.

M.Sc.(Microbiology)

The M.Sc.(Microbiology) course is a full time four semesters Master of Science of Microbiology. This degree is primarily focused on subjects such as software, databases, and networking.
